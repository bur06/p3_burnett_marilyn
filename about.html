About Us

Candace Hurman founded this company in 2020 to use her extensive swim background to help others. Candace acquired many accolades during her 12 years of competitive swimming. She served as a lifeguard for five years and has taught group and private swim lessons for almost 30 years.

Water Safety is #1

Candace accesses each swimmer to apply tried methods at each stage. She recommends two lessons each week with reinforcement of skills at home. Lessons range from 20 to 30 minutes that incorporate fun instruction.
